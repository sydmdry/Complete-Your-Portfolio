Get To Know Me

I have always had a tendency to gravitate towards what is not expected of me. I want to make a statement, but I want that statement to cause movement. When looking for a career, I was very particular about choosing a path that I could use to influence change. Most would not immediately think of coding as my obvious career choice, and neither did I. After discovering so many inspiring tech companies dedicated towards using programming for good; from addressing social issues to environmental solutions, I was ready to take the leap and eager to take part.

I completed the Full-Stack Web Developer program at Thinkful where I became proficient in:

  • HTML/CSS
  • JavaScript
  • SQL

and other associated frameworks including Node.js, React, jQuery, and PostgreSQL.
